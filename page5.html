TEAMS SOCIETIES EVENTS

Events Timeline

12 March 2018

Project Presentation on Applications of RADAR

A PowerPoint Presentation Competition on Applications of RADAR was conducted for 4th year EC students. They were judged by Dr. Falgun Thakkar and Prof. Mayank Ardeshana. There were 9 groups and each group elaborated on various topics related to “Application of RADAR”. Various topics that they covered were Weather Radar, Global Positioning System, Bistatic Radar, Applications of RADAR in military, remote sensing, ATC, aircraft safety and navigation, ship safety and spaceships, SONAR.

5-9 March 2018

Photoshop SIG

SIG on Photoshop is designed to get you acquainted with the basics of Photoshop which helps one to showcase their creativity in the form of different banners, posters, flyers etc. People usually come across many creative ideas, but very few know the basics of converting them in to real posters. The SIG, with the motive of guiding the students to develop their creative minds and transforming it in the form of posters was conducted by Mr. Mayank Limbachiya EE 4th year and Ms. Ashka Soni CP 4th year GCET.

21 February 2018

PCB Designing Workshop

A workshop on PCB Designing was organized for 4th semester EC students. It was conducted in 2 sessions. The first session focussed on the construction of PCB i.e. how the PCB is being constructed, what are the steps for the construction of PCB etc. The second session was totally practical, students were given live demonstration of what they were learning. “EAGLE” is the software which was used for the live demonstration.

16-17 February 2018

Workshop of Microsoft Azure

A two-day workshop on Microsoft AZURE for the students of G H Patel College of Engineering & Technology, Sardar Patel Educational Campus & Madhuben and Bhanubhai Patel Women Institute of Engineering. The motive of the talk was to create an awareness about the Imagine Cup and learn the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE. The session on Introduction to Imagine Cup was conducted by Mr. Venkatesh Sarvasiddhi & Ms. Osheen Chavhan. The following session about the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE was conducted by Mr. Chaitanya Dandvate.
