IEEE

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. There are more than 423,000 IEEE members in over 160 countries around the world. IEEE members are engineers, scientists, and allied professionals whose technical interests are rooted in electrical and computer sciences, engineering, and related disciplines. The highest grade of membership, IEEE Fellow, is attained through nomination by peers and approval by the IEEE Board of Directors for distinction in the profession.

IEEE GCET SB

IEEE GCET Student Branch was approved by IEEE Headquarters, NewYork on the 28th February, 2006. The founding branch consisted of total 29 student members and 2 executive members. IEEE GCET Student Branch is the 7th IEEE Student Branch in Gujarat Section, positioned in IEEE's Asia Pacific Region (IEEE Region 10). The IEEE GCET Student Branch is well supported by the Charutar Vidhya Mandal and our beloved principal Dr. Himanshu Soni to provide a worthy platform to amateurs and beginners. Currently, IEEE GCET SB is led by Dr. Hardik Pathak, Branch counsellor and Mr. Saurabh H Sachdev, Chairperson, IEEE GCET SB with his team of highly energetic and expert team of IEEE volunteers. Advancing technology for humanity, IEEE GCET SB has always contributed in improvising technology and professionalism among students.

GCET

GCET is the one of the prominent college for Engineering in Gujarat. It was ranked 23rd among top 100 Engineering Institutes in India by the DATAQUEST 2016-17. It is located in Vallabh Vidyanagar and affiliated with Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad. It is managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM). Vallabh Vidyanagar, named after the late Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel - the 'Iron Man of India' - has over the last fifty years been developed into a well-known seat of learning. The educational township, established in 1946, today provides pre-primary to postgraduate education. The presence of Sardar Patel University, here has boosted the pace of progress of educational activities.
