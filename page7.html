Microsoft Azure

A two-day workshop on Microsoft AZURE for the students of G H Patel College of Engineering & Technology, Sardar Patel Educational Campus & Madhuben and Bhanubhai Patel Women Institute of Engineering. The motive of the talk was to create an awareness about the Imagine Cup and learn the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE. The session on Introduction to Imagine Cup was conducted by Mr. Venkatesh Sarvasiddhi & Ms. Osheen Chavhan. The following session about the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE was conducted by Mr. Chaitanya Dandvate.

Web Designing SIG

SIG on Web Development is designed to start you on a path towards future studies in web development and design, no matter how little experience or technical knowledge you currently have. The web is a very big place, and being a typical internet user, one probably visits several websites every day, whether for business, entertainment or education. The SIG, with the motive of imparting such coding skills in the participants, was conducted by Yash Mehta, IT 4th year & Vishvam Thakkar, CP 4th year GCET.

Expert Talk on jQuery & Bootstrap

An expert talk on “jQuery and Bootstrap” for third year students of Information Technology department. The motive of this talk was to educate the students about the use of jQuery and Bootstrap in Web Development. The speaker for this session was Mr. Yogesh Pande, a passionate Microsoft .Net developer. He concluded that self-employment can also be possible if you know the basics of Bootstrap & jQuery. At the end, memento was presented to Mr. Yogesh Pande by the Head of Department, Dr. Nikhil Gondaliya. In total, 53 students of Information Technology participated in this talk.
