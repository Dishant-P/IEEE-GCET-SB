GCET has become a prominent place of learning over the past thirteen years, as it has the pride of bringing forth several dignitaries in the engineering discipline.It is located in Vallabh Vidyanagar and affiliated with Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad. It is managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM). Founded in 1996, with two disciplines, Information Technology and Chemical Engineering, today GCET runs 8 disciplines including computer engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication, Mechanical, Mechatronics and Civil Engineering.
