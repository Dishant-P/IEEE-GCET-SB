SEMINAR ON ENTREPRENEURSHIP

DATE: 1st February, 2019
VENUE: Seminar Hall, GCET
PARTICIPANTS: 40
Seminar was organized on “Entrepreneurship” for third year electrical students. The session was conducted by Mr.Dip Dhingani,
The session was about the funding issues, business decisions and lack of market demand. He introduced some of the basic concepts of entrepreneurship like financial investment, hiring the right people for the specialized jobs and the risk factors involved. Altogether, it was a very informative session.
