Microsoft Azure

A two-day workshop on Microsoft AZURE for the students of G H Patel College of Engineering & Technology, Sardar Patel Educational Campus & Madhuben and Bhanubhai Patel Women Institute of Engineering. The motive of the talk was to create an awareness about the Imagine Cup and learn the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE. The session on Introduction to Imagine Cup was conducted by Mr. Venkatesh Sarvasiddhi & Ms. Osheen Chavhan. The following session about the fundamentals of Microsoft AZURE was conducted by Mr. Chaitanya Dandvate.
