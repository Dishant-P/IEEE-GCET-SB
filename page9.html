GLOBAL PES DAY

DATE: 11th April, 2019
VENUE: GCET
PARTICIPANTS: 60
IEEE GCET SB celebrated “GLOBAL PES DAY” on 11th April, 2019 in the honour of the 2nd PES Day which marks the completion of 11 years of new PES name. On 22nd April, 2008. The Power Engineering Society changed its name to the Power & Energy Society after a final vote from the PES members.
Prof.Nirmal Kumar C Nair, Associate Professor of Auckland University, were the Speaker of Eminence for the session. He conducted his session on “Protection and Safety of Power Systems with Highly Renewable Distributed Energy Resources” The event ended at 01:30 pm with a cake cutting session.
